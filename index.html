Nairobi, Kenya

Kenya is a country located in East Africa, bordered by Tanzania, Uganda, South Sudan, Ethiopia, and Somalia. Known for its diverse landscapes ranging from savannahs and mountains to lakes and beaches, Kenya is famous for its wildlife, including lions, elephants, and rhinos, found in national parks such as the Maasai Mara. Nairobi, its capital city, serves as a regional hub for commerce, culture, and government. .
